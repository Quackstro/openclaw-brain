## Assessment

**Patriot Select ($3,000) — best value if:**
- Lanai coverage exclusion is acceptable (small attached lanai, ~$3K–6K replacement)
- $88,775 personal property coverage is sufficient
- Want emergency water removal program
- Want option for higher liability ($500K) or animal liability

**Praxis ($3,353) — worth $353 more if:**
- Screen enclosure / lanai coverage matters
- Need higher personal property coverage ($124K vs $89K)
- Have trampolines, pool slides, etc.
- Want premium financing
- Value the 72-hour repair rule and higher emergency measures limit

**Bottom line:** For a small attached lanai, Patriot is likely the better value. The $353 annual savings is guaranteed; the lanai coverage benefit is hypothetical and would take 8–17 years of savings to offset a single claim (before deductible). The stronger Praxis arguments are the personal property bump and higher special limits.
